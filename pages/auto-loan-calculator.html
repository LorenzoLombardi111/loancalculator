How much interest will you pay on a car loan?

On a $35,000 auto loan at 7% for 5 years, you will pay about $6,584 in total interest. That means the car actually costs you $41,584. Shorter loan terms reduce total interest significantly: the same loan over 3 years costs only $3,845 in interest, saving you $2,739. Use freeloancalc.app to compare different terms and find the sweet spot between monthly payment and total cost.

Should I make extra payments on my car loan?

Extra payments go directly toward your principal balance, reducing total interest and shortening your loan. Adding just $100/month to a $35,000 5-year auto loan at 7% saves about $800 in interest and pays off the loan 10 months early. Enter any extra amount in freeloancalc.app to see your specific savings instantly.

What is a good interest rate for a car loan?

Auto loan rates vary by credit score and term. In 2026, rates typically range from 4% to 12%. New cars generally get lower rates than used cars. Use freeloancalc.app to compare how different rates affect your total cost.

Should I choose a 3-year or 5-year car loan?

A shorter loan has higher monthly payments but less total interest. A 5-year loan on $35,000 at 7% costs $3,651 in interest; a 3-year loan costs only $2,121. Use freeloancalc.app to compare both and find the right balance.
